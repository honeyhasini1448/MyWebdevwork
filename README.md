After completing my AP EAPCET examination, I spent my holidays exploring different career options before choosing my college and course. During that time, I watched many YouTube videos about computers, programming, robotics, artificial intelligence, and technology.

As I learned more, I became fascinated by web development.

I have always enjoyed creative activities like drawing and designing, so building websites felt like the perfect combination of creativity and technology. That curiosity motivated me to start learning HTML and CSS through free YouTube tutorials. Later, I completed a web development course to strengthen my fundamentals and continued building projects on my own.
